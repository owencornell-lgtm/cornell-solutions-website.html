Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy explains how Cornell Solutions LLC ("we," "us") handles information in connection with our website and services.

1. Information we collect

  • Information you give us: your name, business email, company details, and anything you share when you book a call or become a client.
  • Campaign data: prospect and lead information you provide or authorize us to source on your behalf in order to run your campaigns.
  • Usage data: standard analytics about how our website is used, such as pages viewed and general device information.

2. How we use it

We use information to provide and operate our services, run and improve your campaigns, communicate with you, and meet legal and accounting obligations. We do not sell your personal information.

3. Service providers

We rely on trusted third-party tools to deliver our services — for example, scheduling, email sending and warming, CRM and database, payment processing, and communications platforms. These providers process information only as needed to perform their function and under their own terms.

4. Prospect data

When we run outbound on your behalf, we process business-contact information to deliver your campaigns. We honor opt-out and unsubscribe requests and operate to comply with applicable anti-spam and data-protection laws.

5. Data security & retention

We take reasonable measures to protect information and retain it only as long as needed to provide the services or as required by law. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Your choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you can unsubscribe from our communications at any time. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date reflects the most recent revision.
